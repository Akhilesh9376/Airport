Airports Authority of India (AAI) is responsible for creation development and management of Air Cargo Terminals for processing of international air cargo (export, import , transshipment and courier consignment) at international and domestic airports in India. The Air cargo Terminals managed by AAI are at Chennai, Kolkata , Guwahati, Lucknow, Coimbatore, Portblair, Jaipur, Mangalore, Amritsar and Trichy Airports.
